What Kind of Employee Works for Comcentia

Comcentia projects require IT professionals who thrive in a dynamic customer-focused environment. If your skills include the latest programming languages and development processes, and if your experience includes working in flexible and demanding results-oriented teams, then upload your résumee. Thank you for your interest in working for Comcentia!

Résumé Suggestions

Before you upload your résumé, please follow these suggestions for providing information that Comcentia considers relevant to our hiring process:

  • Your work history should describe your impact on each employer. Don't just list what was in your job description. If your contribution made a difference, let us know. Your past performance is very important.
  • List all relevant training and certifications in addition to your formal education. That process training or programming class might match our needs!
  • List all relevant skills—be sure to include skills that are required for the position.
  • Provide your contact information including your email address and phone number.
  • Add a hyperlink to your LinkedIn profile, especially if you have recommendations and endorsements from relevant past clients and employers.
