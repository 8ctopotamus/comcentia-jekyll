Business Intelligence Services

Whether you’re capturing it in a database now or not, your business both relies on and generates information. With purposeful compilation, measurement and reporting, you can analyze this data to help make better, more informed more insightful business decisions.

Comcentia Business Intelligence and Software Integration helps transform such data mining from a constant stream of disruptive requests to the IT Department into simple, self-service reporting functions for your organization’s decision-makers. Comcentia’s expertise in integrating financial and operational applications — sales, payroll, billing, logistics, project management — is the key to leveraging data from across the enterprise and turning it into true, actionable business intelligence.

From production to sales, time-tracking to purchasing, staffing to inventory management, Comcentia can integrate your organization’s data and software, complete with easy-to-use dashboards and visual reporting, to help you drive better decision-making for smoother operations and enhanced productivity.

Business Intelligence Maintenance

Our team continually reviews your organization's business intelligence system for compliance with government standards and best practices and then updates and upgrades your BI software.

Microsoft SQL Reporting, SSRS, SSIS

We develop effective data management and reporting solutions using Microsoft SQL Reporting of business data, SQL Server Reporting Services, and SQL Server Integration Services. These software tools allow scalability and flexibility when working with business intelligence data.

Business Process Optimization and Integration

Comcentia analyzes existing business processes and system integration to determine the most efficient use of your software and equipment resources. Have you outgrown your legacy systems and databases? Do you provide a common and consistent interface to your users? Are your procedures still best practices?
