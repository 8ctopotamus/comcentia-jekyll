Large Business Enterprise Software Development

Your business relies on information and processes being used throughout your organization. Comcentia makes managing enterprise software applications simpler and more efficient.

With a track record for delivering programs on time and on budget, Comcentia provides solutions for enterprise portal and intranet applications; web enablement or conversion of legacy applications to modern platforms; and mobile and content management system (CMS) implementation.

Enterprise Application Support

To fully benefit from technology, it has to work as intended. Anything less detracts from productivity. And the frustration of working with technology that performs at less than optimal functionality can cause even worse problems, because it can provide employees with an easy excuse for further deterioration of productivity.

That’s why Comcentia Application Support is so valuable. When you engage Comcentia to manage your applications, whether they were developed specifically for you or bought off the shelf, Comcentia will:

  • Plan and install all software updates on a schedule that works for you
  • Resolve bugs or errors on a priority basis
  • Program changes to preferences and options as needed
  • Program application-generated reports to your strategic needs
  • Scrub and format data to support accurate reporting for confident decision-making

In short, Comcentia Application Support optimizes your software’s performance, so your employees and your business can perform at their highest level of efficiency.
