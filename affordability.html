Affordability

Lower overhead translates into project Affordabil-IT, Viabil-IT

Because Comcentia is based in the affordable, yet Top 25, US market of Milwaukee, Wisconsin — as opposed to expensive major markets like New York, London or Silicon Valley — we have access to savvy, highly-trained software engineers who have chosen to live in a vibrant city with a lower cost of living and an unparalleled quality of life. Comcentia, too, benefits from lower overhead.

Consequently, when you engage Comcentia, you don’t have to compromise quality for affordability. Which simply makes some initiatives absolutely viable — even though project estimates from other firms might have made you believe they were impossible based on your budget.
