Since 2006, the Comcentia team of experienced veteran software engineers and business professionals has has been focused on providing the best IT solutions for businesses of any size.

Our vision is to provide IT software development and consulting that delivers projects on time, on budget, and exceeding your expectations. Our successful results demonstrate that Comcentia IT solutions in the public and private sectors are purposeful, scalable, agile, on time, and within budget.

Our architecture expertise includes Assets Management Systems, Financial Systems, Enterprise Content Management, Microsoft SharePoint, CRM Systems, Microsoft Dynamics, Web Development, Enterprise Application Architecture and Integration, Business Intelligence, .NET Development, and Business Process Optimization.

Comcentia designs and implements large-scale software applications in many languages and platforms including Java, JSP/Servlet, JDBC, Swing, JavaScript, EJB, Websphere Studio, Brio Business Intelligence tools, Crystal Report, Select Business Solutions Design tools, C#, Visual Basic.Net, ASP.Net Form and MVC, C/C++, Oracle, and SQL Server.

Comcentia is a certified minority-owned, disadvantaged, and 8(a) business enterprise. We are a member for the Society for Information Management Wisconsin Chapter (SIM), Metropolitan Milwaukee Association of Commerce (MMAC), The Business Council (TBC), and Northwest Milwaukee Chamber of Commerce (NWMCC).
